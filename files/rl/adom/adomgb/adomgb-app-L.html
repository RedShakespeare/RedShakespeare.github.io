Appendix L – Locations – complete list

Abbr is the dungeon level displayed in the lower right corner of the screen, DL is the danger level, tttn is the top trap type number. For locations with more levels, add +5 for subsequent levels wrapping around 11 (so 7+5=0). For any Caverns of Chaos levels the DL equals the dungeon level and tttn = (level×5 - 1) mod 12. For ID: tttn = (level×5 + 1) mod 12. So, for example, on D: 1 the trap that you would find in the top left hand corner of the screen, if you could get there and make one, is an arrow trap (trap number 4). Within these lists the ttt just increases by 5 each time. Trap table was removed after 1.1.1, so locations added in R+ or R++ have it there instead of tttn.
