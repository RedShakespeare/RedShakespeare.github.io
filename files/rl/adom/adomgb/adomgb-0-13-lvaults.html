Lesser vaults

The numbers 1-4 represent monsters in order of increasing commonness within themed lesser vaults. In mixed vaults, these are replaced by random monsters. The / character represents a passage (secret or otherwise). The edges of the layouts will vary somewhat, depending on the overall dungeon layout.
