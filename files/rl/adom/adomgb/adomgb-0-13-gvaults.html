Greater vaults

The numbers 1-7 represent monsters in order of increasing commonness. The $ character represents a grand treasure. The / character represents a passage (secret or otherwise). The edges of the layouts will vary somewhat, depending on the overall dungeon layout. Layouts in the second row do not appear in 1.1.1 and earlier versions.
