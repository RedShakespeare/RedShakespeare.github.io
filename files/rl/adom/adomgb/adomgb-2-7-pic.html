This level is heavily and randomly trapped in the labyrinth on the left.
It contains a large variety of undead in the chamber on the right.

Monsters:

Z - Griff Bloodax, the undead chaos dwarven berserker
@ - Nonnak, the master necromancer
Y - Bone Golem, guarding the Elemental Gauntlets

Symbols:

+ - Griff's grave, where the Sword of Nonnak can be found
# - secret passages
