This particular map shows the result when a non-Wizard enters
the Library. There are scrolls located in the two large rooms. 
Wizards and Necromancers will find only spellbooks.

Friendly inhabitant:

G - Ghost Librarian

There will be a random dangerous monster guarding every spellbook and scroll.

Symbols:

" - spellbooks
? - scrolls
] - climbing set
# - secret passages
