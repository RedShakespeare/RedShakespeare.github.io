The color and location of the pools is random. 6-12 pools is typical.

Monsters:

h - Kherab, the dwarven artificer
Y - steel golems

Also randomly found:

[ - animated armors
h - dwarven chaos knights
Y - steel horrors
Y - steel zombies

Symbols:

+ - closed doors
# - secret passages
[ - (bottom left) Crown of Science and a steel golem Y
& - forges
$ - gold
[ - armor
( - weapons
0 - colored pools
> - the down stariracase leads to CoC level 32 or 33
