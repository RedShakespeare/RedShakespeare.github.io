The staircase in the central part of the map leads to the Ogre Cave after the
PC has received the quest.

Friendly inhabitants:

@ - Ruun, the priest (always near the altar)
@ - Waldenbrook, the shopkeeper (always in his shop)
m - mimic (always in Waldenbrook's shop, hostile)
h - Garth, the dwarven weaponmaster (always in his training hall)
h - Glod, the dwarven smith (always near his forge)
h - Thrundarr, the ancient dwarf (always standing on the lever in his room)
h - Dwarven Mystic (always in his sealed room)
r - Demented Ratling
h - dwarven children
h - dwarven elite guardians (always in front of Thrundarr's room)
h - dwarven guardians
h - dwarves (male and female) and Torgall (always in the Green Griffon Inn)
@ - Bart, the grizzled gladiator (always in the Green Griffon Inn)
