Friendly inhabitants:

@ - Gaab'Baay, the old crone (wanders after secret passage is discovered)
@ - Hawkslayer, the burly adventurer
@ - Leggot, the shopkeeper (always in his shop)
@ - Malicious Doctor (wanders if door is open)
@ - Oracle (never moves)
t - farmer (commonly generated but not guaranteed; location random)
r - ratling trader (commonly generated but not guaranteed; location random)
O - Ogre Guardian (never moves)
x - random low-level hostile monsters (always in jail)
r - ratling guardians (never move)
@ - Grizzled Dwarven Veteran

There will also be some random monsters, some of which may well be hostile.

Symbols:

+ - closed doors
# - secret passages
? - cursed scroll of corruption removal and strange message
! - potion of ultra healing
